The old money style for men is defined by timeless elegance, understated luxury, and impeccable tailoring. It features classic pieces like well-fitted navy or charcoal suits, crisp dress shirts, and polished leather shoes. Accessories are minimal yet refined, including silk ties, gold cufflinks, and vintage timepieces. This aesthetic values quality over trends, exuding sophistication and quiet confidence.
