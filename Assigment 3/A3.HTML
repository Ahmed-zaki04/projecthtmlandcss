Freelancing: A Modern Way of Working

In today's world, freelancing has become one of the most popular ways of working. A freelancer
is a self employed individual who offers services to clients without being committed to a long term
contract. Freelancing provides flexibility, independence, and the opportunity to work on various projects across different industries.

Popular Freelancing Platforms

There are several online platforms where freelancers can find work, including:

  • Upwork
  • Fiverr
  • Freelancer
  • Toptal
  • PeoplePerHour
