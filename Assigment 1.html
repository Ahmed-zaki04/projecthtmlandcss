The Journey of Web Development

Chapter 1: The Dream Begins


Ahmed, a young and ambitious student, had always been fascinated by the internet. He
wondered how websites were built and dreamed of creating his own. One day, he decided to
start learning Web Development.

Chapter 2: The First Steps


Ahmed discovered that web development consists of three main technologies:


HTML: for structure CSS: for design JavaScript: for interactivity

Excited, he started writing his first lines of HTML code, creating a simple webpage with a title and a paragraph.
